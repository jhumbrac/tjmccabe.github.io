Paegel's Pottery

Paegel hopes you enjoy his pot as much as he does


Michael Paegel lives in Mission Viejo, California and is exceedingly passionate about conceptualizing and producing his own unique brand of pottery. He also happens to be a hardworking childcare professional working towards a teaching credential.


Michael is happy to accept commissions of all sizes of pieces such as

  • Bowls
  • Cups
  • Vases
  • Pitchers
  • Flower pots
  • Animal likenesses
And many more!
